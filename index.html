Security First

Security is our top priority. That’s why our tokenomics have been carefully designed with the following key factors:

Scarcity: To prevent inflationary conditions, the total supply is strategically limited.

Burn Mechanism: A built-in burn mechanism ensures deflationary tendencies, meaning that as the total supply gradually decreases over time, the value of individual coins can increase.

Team Token Lock: All team tokens are subject to a linear vesting schedule and will be locked for 90 days. This completely eliminates the risk of a pump-and-dump scenario.

Transaction Limit: A maximum of 2% of the total tokens held by an individual can be sold per transaction. This significantly reduces price volatility and stabilizes market movements.

Testnet Implementation: Before launching on the mainnet, AI-Bull undergoes multiple rigorous test cycles on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) Testnet to ensure security and functionality. After a successful BSC mainnet launch, we will consider expanding to other major networks such as Ethereum.

Important Notice: At launch, AI-Bull will initially be available exclusively on the BSC Mainnet.
